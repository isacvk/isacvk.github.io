HEALTHBRIDGE

A landing page for an imaginary startup built with the use of chatGPT (content) and midjourney ai (images).

Introduction

A website landing page design along with little bit of branding for my imaginary startup called "Health Brdige". This startup's aim is to connect patients with doctors via video conference or chat or voice call.

Branding

I asked chatGPT to create some brand names for my startup and it gave me a bunch of ideas. I decided to call my imaginary startup - Health Bridge.

The name "Bridge Health" captures the essence of the company's mission and value proposition, and suggests that the company is a bridge that connects people with the healthcare they need.

Tagline - “Bridge the gap between you and better health”

Color Scheme

Green is often associated with health and wellness, and could be a good choice to convey the company's focus on healthcare.

Orange is often associated with energy and positivity, and could be a good choice to convey a friendly and approachable brand image.

Together, green and orange can create a balanced and harmonious color palette, and could be effective for creating a cohesive and professional visual identity.

Logo

I tried creating a simple logo that conveys the message of the company and also looks modern and suitable for different screen size and materials.

Because of the time limit I didn’t work on the logo too much. It’s far from a more polished brand identity
