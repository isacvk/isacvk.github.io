DREAMHOME

KK resort is a resort in Coorg, Karnataka. In this project I helped them create a seamless booking experience.
Progress: Work in progress

Background

Countless room booking services already exist in the market like Airbnb, makemytrip. This resort was also offering their service in these sites. For them one of the main problems was they offered trucking services but had no option to promote or let their clients know that they had these services.

Problems

In that area a lot of resorts are there, and all of them are on sites like makemytrip or goibibo so standing out in the competition is hard.

This resort also had trucking services and there was no option to promote or let the clients know about their other service on these websites.

Market Research

Since similar services already exist in the market I did a research on those services and took note of really helpful features and also features I wanted as a customer. I did this mainly to find out their most significant pain points, so we bypass them on our way to creating a design.

Research findings and Personas

During my research I realized that I found it annoying to login or create an account just to see if a room is available on a specific date.

Low fidelity wireframe and user flow

Then I created a low fidelity mockup to make sure my client is happy with the flow.

High fidelity wireframe

Created a high fidelity wireframe and got feedback from potential clients.

Design and prototype

Created a design and prototype and got feedback from the users and updated it according to the needs.
